## Course snapshot (from UiO page)
- **Credits/level:** 10 ECTS, bachelor level, Autumn offering (taught as resources allow).
- **Language:** English (may be Norwegian if all agree).

- **Official info & schedules:** see the course page link above.

## Topics covered
- Probability measures and σ-algebras
- Random variables and ways to describe their distributions
- Conditional expectation and filtrations
- Convergence of random variables; laws of large numbers
- Characteristic functions and the central limit theorem
- Discrete-time martingales

## Expected learning outcomes
- Understand measure-theoretic definitions of probability, independence, random variable, stochastic process, and conditional expectation.
- Describe distributions of random variables in multiple equivalent ways.
- Work with limits of sequences of random variables using standard convergence notions.
- Use filtrations/conditional expectations to represent information and manipulate discrete-time martingales.

## Admission and recommended background
